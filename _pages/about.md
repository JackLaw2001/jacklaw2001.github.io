I am a PhD candidate in Computer Science at Newcastle University. Before I came to Newcastle University in September 2023, I obtained my Bachelor's degree in Mathematics and Applied Mathematics from Hunan Normal University.

During my undergraduate studies, I was selected to join the Shicheng Honor Class (now Shicheng Hornor College, 世承书院) and joined Professor Xiao Lin's research group. In my sophomore year, I started to study the application of zeroing neural networks in multi-agent consensus control. Then, I received systematic scientific research training under the guidance of Professor Xiao Lin.

At present, my research interests include brain, epilepsy and neural networks.
